<p><strong>The Cryogenic Turboexpanders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-shaft Turbins</li><li>Double-shaft Turbins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cryogenic turboexpanders are classified into single-shaft and double-shaft turbines based on their design and operational characteristics. Single-shaft turbines consist of a single rotating shaft that integrates both the expander and generator, offering simplicity and compactness, ideal for small-scale applications. In contrast, double-shaft turbines feature two separate shafts, allowing for greater flexibility and efficiency in larger systems, as they can optimize power generation and expand different gases simultaneously. Both types play crucial roles in cryogenic processes for energy applications.</p></p>
